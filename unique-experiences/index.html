Masai Mara Hot Air
Balloon Safari

Ascend over the theater of the wild as the sun breaks the horizon

Overview

Drift silently over the iconic plains of the Masai Mara. Rising before the dawn, watch the wild awaken from a vantage point hundreds of feet above. Witness majestic wildlife herds casting long morning shadows across the savanna before touching down for a luxurious champagne breakfast in the bush.

The Experience

The morning begins in the quiet dark. As the burners roar to life, you lift off into the cool air just as the sun breaks over the horizon. Watch the shadows of elephants and wildebeest sweep across the plains from hundreds of feet up. Upon landing, a white-linen table awaits you in the middle of the savannah for a traditional champagne bush breakfast.

Duration Morning (Approx. 3 Hours)
Investment Custom Tailored

What's Included

  • Pre-dawn transfer from your camp to launch site
  • Briefing by a fully licensed FAA/KCAA commercial pilot
  • Approximately 1-hour flight over the Mara savanna
  • Five-star champagne bush breakfast with chef services
"Drifting silently above the savanna as the sun rose, then landing to find a white-linen champagne breakfast set up in the wild — an unforgettable morning."

— Elena & Victor, Switzerland

Laikipia Equestrian Safaris

Ride alongside the wild, matching the rhythm of the bush

Overview

Forgo the safari vehicle entirely and experience the African wilderness in its purest form. Riding highly trained thoroughbreds, you'll blend seamlessly into the environment, approaching wildlife far closer than any car allows. Explore vast plateaus and track game alongside expert equestrian guides.

The Experience

Forgo the vehicle entirely and blend seamlessly into the wilderness. Riding highly trained thoroughbreds and safari horses, you can approach wildlife far closer than any car allows. Ride across expansive plateaus, track game with expert equestrian guides, and feel the true rhythm of the African bush.

Scenic Helicopter Safaris
of Remote Kenya

The ultimate expression of exclusive, untamed travel

Overview

Soar above Kenya’s most inaccessible and dramatic terrains. Fly low over volcanic ridges, touch down on Mount Kenya's peaks for morning coffee, or explore the unearthly northern desert dunes. Entirely private and customized, this is safari travel elevated to its absolute peak.

The Experience

The ultimate expression of luxury travel. Take off directly from your lodge and fly low over jagged mountain ridges, hidden waterfalls, and volcanic craters. Touch down on the peaks of Mount Kenya for a morning coffee, or explore the surreal, unearthly landscapes of the northern deserts—entirely inaccessible by road.

Duration Full Day or Custom Itineraries
