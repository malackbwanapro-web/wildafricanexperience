Tsavo: The Red Elephant
& Lava Wilderness

A rugged kingdom that feels entirely your own

Overview

A rugged, majestic landscape that feels entirely your own. Tsavo is famous for its massive "red elephants," dust-bathing in the vibrant terracotta soil, and the dramatic Mzima Springs. This package guarantees absolute seclusion and a ruggedly elegant footprint — wildlife in its rawest, most untouched form.

The Experience

Your private safari vehicle and expert guide are at your disposal from dawn until dusk. Track leopards through the acacia trees, marvel at the ancient Shetani lava flows, and enjoy sundowners overlooking the endless blue volcanic horizons. Every moment is tailored to your pace.

Duration 3 Days / 2 Nights
Investment From €900 per person

What's Included

  • Private, luxury 4×4 Land Cruiser with dedicated expert guide
  • Luxury camp or lodge accommodation (2 nights)
  • All Tsavo National Park fees
  • Customised daily game drives at your preferred pace
  • Gourmet bush meals & signature sundowner experience

Laikipia: Private Conservancies
& Custom Luxury

Designed entirely around your tastes, on your rhythm

Overview

Laikipia is the playground of Africa's most successful conservation stories and its most opulent private estates. Free from the restrictions of national parks, this trip is completely bespoke — designed around your exact tastes and rhythm, and set against a backdrop of sweeping plateaus and ancient rivers.

The Experience

Stepping into Laikipia means escaping the crowds completely. Whether you choose a classic luxury tented camp overlooking a watering hole or an architectural masterpiece of a private villa, your days are defined by choice. Track endangered black rhinos, enjoy night game drives not possible in national parks, and savour world-class hospitality tailored precisely to your preferences.

Duration 3 Days / 2 Nights
Investment Tailored to Your Accommodation

What's Included

  • Private, luxury 4×4 Land Cruiser for all game drives
  • Bespoke itinerary coordination & pre-trip planning
  • Full-board elite accommodation of your choosing
  • Exclusive private conservancy access & night drives

Masai Mara: The Grand Horizon

The quintessential African dream, experienced without compromise

Limited Availability — Great Migration Season

Overview

The quintessential African dream brought to life. Famed for its dense predator populations and the iconic Great Migration, the Masai Mara package is designed for discerning travellers who want to witness the best of the Mara in ultimate comfort and absolute privacy — without a crowd in sight.

The Experience

With your private Land Cruiser, you bypass the masses to find the most intimate wildlife viewings. Spend your days watching prides of lions orchestrate their hunts across the endless golden plains, and your evenings at an exclusive five-star luxury camp where fine dining and canvas-wrapped opulence await beneath an infinite canopy of stars.

Duration 3 Days / 2 Nights
Investment From €1,500 per person sharing

What's Included

  • Private luxury 4×4 Land Cruiser with expert naturalist guide
  • Luxury five-star tented camp accommodation (2 nights)
  • All Masai Mara park & private conservancy fees
  • Premium all-inclusive meals & signature sundowners
  • Early morning & evening game drive access to prime areas
"Watching the wildebeest crossing with our private guide — just the two of us — while the rest of the crowds were miles away. That is WAE."
